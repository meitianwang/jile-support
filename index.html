Privacy

记了吗不收集任何个人数据。所有记账数据仅存储在您的设备本地或您的私人 iCloud 账户中。AI 解析功能通过第三方 API 处理文本，不存储任何用户数据。

Jile does not collect any personal data. All records are stored locally on your device or in your private iCloud account. AI parsing uses a third-party API and does not store any user data.
